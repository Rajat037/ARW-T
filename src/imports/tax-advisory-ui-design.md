Design a modern, professional yet Gen-Z friendly 2D website UI for a tax advisory company that helps content creators, freelancers, and social media influencers manage taxes, GST, and financial compliance.

The website should look trustworthy like a financial advisory firm but visually appealing and modern enough for digital creators. Use smooth motion effects, micro-interactions, and clean layouts.

VISUAL STYLE

* Clean 2D modern SaaS style design
* Minimal but vibrant UI
* Rounded cards and smooth shadows
* Subtle gradients and modern shapes
* Professional but approachable color palette
* Use deep blue or indigo for trust, combined with modern accents like teal, purple, or emerald
* Include subtle motion ideas like hover effects, floating shapes, and scroll reveal animations
* Maintain excellent spacing and visual hierarchy

TARGET USERS

* Social media influencers
* YouTubers
* Instagram creators
* Freelancers
* Digital entrepreneurs
* Gen Z professionals who want simple tax solutions

WEBSITE STRUCTURE

1. HERO SECTION
   Headline: clear value proposition for creators

Example style:
“Taxes Made Simple for Creators & Influencers”

Subheading explaining the service:
Helping creators file taxes, manage GST, and stay compliant without stress.

Include:

* Two CTA buttons (Get Tax Consultation / File My Taxes)
* Abstract financial illustrations or creator-economy visuals
* Subtle animated shapes or gradients
* Clean modern typography

2. TRUST INDICATORS SECTION
   Show credibility using statistics and trust elements.

Example items:
✔ 500+ Creators Served
✔ Expert Chartered Accountants
✔ Secure & Confidential Process
✔ Fast Digital Filing

Use modern stat cards with icons and subtle animations.

3. SERVICES SECTION
   Display services as clean feature cards with icons.

Example cards:
Income Tax Filing for Creators
GST Registration & Compliance
Freelancer Tax Planning
Brand Deal Income Management
Digital Creator Tax Advisory

Use colorful but minimal icons and hover effects.

4. HOW IT WORKS SECTION
   Create a simple 3-step process timeline.

Step 1 – Upload your documents
Step 2 – Our Chartered Accountant reviews your case
Step 3 – We file your taxes and confirm submission

Use simple illustrations or numbered cards.

5. PRICING SECTION
   Display transparent pricing using modern pricing cards.

Example plans:
Creator ITR Filing
Freelancer Tax Package
GST Setup for Creators

Highlight the most popular plan.

6. TESTIMONIAL SECTION
   Show testimonials from creators and freelancers.

Use modern testimonial cards with:

* profile images
* short reviews
* star ratings

7. FINAL CALL TO ACTION
   Encourage users to take action.

Example headline:
“Focus on Creating. We’ll Handle the Taxes.”

Include CTA buttons like:
Get Free Consultation
Start Tax Filing

8. FOOTER
   Clean multi-column footer with:

* About
* Services
* Contact
* Social links
* Privacy policy

DESIGN GOAL
The final UI should look like a modern fintech startup website designed for the creator economy. It should balance trust (important for financial services) with a modern digital aesthetic that appeals to Gen-Z entrepreneurs.
